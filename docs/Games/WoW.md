# Classic WoW TBC

#### Addons
* [ElvUI - Complete UI overhaul to modernize the look of the game](https://www.tukui.org/classic-tbc-addons.php?id=2)
* [Questie - Quest helper addon, marks quests on the minimap](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/questie)
* [OmniCC - Shows cooldown counter on action bars](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/omni-cc)
* [GTFO - Alerts you when you're standing in something you shouldn't be](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/gtfo)
* [Classic Cast Bars - Shows castbars for enemy players and monsters](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/classiccastbars)
* [Leatrix Plus - Adds a bunch of QoL improvements](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/leatrix-plus-bcc)
* [Leatrix Maps - Adds map QoL improvements](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/leatrix-maps-bcc)
* [Deadly Boss Mods - Dungeon and raid encounter helper](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/deadly-boss-mods)
* [Details - Damage and fight stats](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/details)
* [Atlas Loot - Shows all availble items in the game](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/atlaslootclassic)
* [Vendor Price - Shows vendor price in tooltip](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/vendor-price)
* [Quartz - Customizable cast bar](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/quartz)
* [Classic Aura Durations - Shows timers on enemy buffs](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/classicauradurations)
* [Attune - Tracks TBC raid attunements](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/attune)
* [Titan Panel - Customizable UI panel to show various stats](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/titan-panel-classic)
* [KuiNameplates - Enemy nameplate replacement, cleaner and easier to see](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/kuinameplates)
* [Postal - Mailbox enhancement](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/postal)
* [Hunter Swing Timer - Adds a swing timer specific to Hunter, useful for timing shots](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/weaponswingtimer-sixxfix)
* [What's Training - Shows you what you can learn at the class trainer](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/whats-training)
* [aux - Auction house UI overhaul](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/aux)
* [Weak Auras - Basically an addon that allows you to easily make addons in game. Necessity.](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/weakauras-2/files)
* [Druid Bar - Shows mana bar when in form](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/druid-bar-classic)
* [LetMeCast - automatically dismounts you when trying to cast](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/letmecast)
